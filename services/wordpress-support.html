The Problem with Unmaintained WordPress Sites

WordPress requires regular maintenance to stay secure and perform well. Without it, your website faces:

  • Security vulnerabilities - Outdated plugins and themes are hackers' favorite targets
  • Slow loading times - Poor performance drives visitors away and hurts SEO
  • Downtime risks - No backups means losing everything if something goes wrong
  • Broken functionality - Plugin conflicts can break your site without warning
