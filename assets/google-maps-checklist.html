Google Maps Optimization Checklist

The exact 27-point system we use to rank Ghanaian businesses #1

By Gabochie Marketing | marketing.gabochie.com

How to use this checklist: Work through each section in order. Check off items as you complete them. Focus on the high-priority items first (marked with red/yellow borders). This checklist takes approximately 2-3 hours to complete for a new business.
1 Profile Foundation (Critical)
  • Claim and verify your Google Business Profile
    Go to business.google.com and complete verification via phone or postcard
  • Choose the exact right primary category
    This is the #1 ranking factor. Be specific: "Ghanaian Restaurant" beats "Restaurant"
  • Add 5-9 relevant additional categories
    Include services you offer: "Catering," "Event Venue," "Takeaway Restaurant"
  • Enter exact NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
    Match this exactly across all online platforms. Use local Ghana format.
  • Set accurate business hours including holidays
    Update for Ramadan, Christmas, and local holidays. Mark temporary closures.
2 Content Optimization
  • Write keyword-rich business description (750 chars)
    Include: what you do, where you serve (Accra, Kumasi, etc.), unique selling points
  • Add all services with descriptions
    List each service with 2-3 sentence description and pricing if applicable
  • Add products (if applicable)
    Include photos, prices, and descriptions for key products
  • Add attributes (women-led, outdoor seating, etc.)
    Check all that apply - these appear as badges in search results
  • Set service area (if you travel to customers)
    Add specific neighborhoods/areas you serve within Ghana
3 Photo Strategy
  • Upload logo (square, 720x720 minimum)
    Clear, professional logo that displays well as thumbnail
  • Add cover photo showcasing your business
    Best exterior shot or team photo - this appears at top of profile
  • Upload minimum 10 interior/exterior photos
    Show entrance, parking, seating, products, and team at work
  • Add photos to specific services/products
    Each service should have representative images
  • Post weekly photo updates
    Fresh content signals active business to Google algorithm
  • Geotag all photos with your location
    Before uploading, ensure photos have GPS coordinates embedded
4 Review Generation System
  • Create direct review link
    Generate at https://g.page/linkgenerator - bookmark this URL
  • Create review request template (SMS/WhatsApp)
    Template: "Hi [Name], thanks for choosing [Business]. If happy with our service, would you mind leaving a quick review? [LINK] It helps other Ghanaians find us. Thank you!"
  • Set up review request automation
    Send request 24-48 hours after service completion
  • Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
    Thank positive reviewers, professionally address negative ones
  • Aim for 10+ reviews in first month
    Volume matters early on - ask your best customers first
💡 Pro Tip for Ghana Businesses

Many customers won't leave reviews because they don't have Gmail accounts. Offer to help them create one, or ask them to use their existing Google account (many have YouTube accounts linked to Gmail).

5 Local Citations & Backlinks
  • List on Ghana Yellow Pages (yellowpages.com.gh)
    Ensure NAP exactly matches Google profile
  • Create Facebook Business Page with matching NAP
    Link back to your website and Google profile
  • Get listed on industry-specific Ghana directories
    e.g., EatOut Ghana (restaurants), Tonaton (general), Jumia (retail)
  • Add business to Apple Maps
    Submit at mapsconnect.apple.com - growing user base in Ghana
6 Engagement & Posts
  • Create first Google Post (Update/Offer/Event)
    Include photo, 150-300 words, clear call-to-action button
  • Set posting schedule: minimum 1x per week
    Posts expire after 7 days, so consistency is key
  • Enable messaging and respond within 15 minutes
    Turn on Google Business Profile messaging, set up notifications
  • Answer all Q&A questions on your profile
    Pre-populate common questions if none exist yet
🎯 Next Steps After Completing This Checklist
  1. Wait 2-4 weeks for changes to take effect in rankings
  2. Track your position for key search terms weekly
  3. Monitor Insights tab in Google Business Profile for views/actions
  4. Set up monthly photo posting reminder
  5. Review and update checklist quarterly
